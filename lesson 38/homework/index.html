Mercedes-Benz G-Class

The G-Class originated in the late 1970s as a rugged, purpose-built off-roader intended for military and expedition use. Early models emphasized go-anywhere capability, mechanical simplicity, and durability. Over time, the vehicle transitioned from a utilitarian workhorse to a luxury SUV, while retaining its distinctive boxy silhouette and exceptional off-road competence.
